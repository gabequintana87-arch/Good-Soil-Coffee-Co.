Spiritual Direction

Ceceilia

Spiritual Director · Contemplative Guide · DFW & Virtual

Hi, I’m Ceceilia — a Spiritual Director, wife, mother, and contemplative companion who walks with people into a deeper awareness of God’s presence in their everyday lives.

Spiritual Direction is a holy and unhurried practice. In our sessions, I offer a listening, prayerful space where you can slow down and notice where God is moving — in your joys, your struggles, your questions, and your longings.

My heart is to help you cultivate an intimate, attentive relationship with God that grounds every other part of your life. Whether you’re navigating a season of transition, dryness, or deep searching, you don’t have to find your way alone.

Trained Spiritual Director

Certified through a recognized spiritual direction formation program in the contemplative Christian tradition.

Contemplative Rooting

Drawing from the desert fathers and mothers, Celtic spirituality, Ignatian practice, and the Anglican tradition.

What We’ll Explore

Attentiveness to God · Prayer & silence · Seasons of transition · Spiritual discernment · Interior freedom

Vocational Coaching

Gabe

ICF Certified Vocational Coach · Pastor · DFW & Virtual

Hey, I’m Gabe — a pastor, husband, father of 5, foster-adoptive advocate, musician, and ICF Certified Vocational Coach based in the DFW area.

My driving force is simple: I believe everyone is wired with a particular set of gifts, and that what they bring to the table is meant to make the world a better place. Most of us just haven’t had anyone help us see it clearly or put it to work in a meaningful way.

This work is for everyone with a sense of calling — whether you’re a pastor discerning your next season of ministry, a marketplace professional wondering if your work can mean more, or someone starting from scratch trying to figure out what you’re made for. Vocational coaching isn’t about me telling you what to do — I’ll ask the right questions, listen actively, and help you see a perspective you might not have considered. Every great athlete needs a coach. Together, we’ll help you go further, faster.

ICF Certified Coach

International Coaching Federation — the gold standard in professional coaching.

Kingdom-Focused

For pastors, ministry leaders, and marketplace professionals alike — your vocation is a Kingdom calling, wherever you’re placed.

What We’ll Work On

Strengths discovery · Career pivots · Calling & purpose · Job search strategy · Leadership development
