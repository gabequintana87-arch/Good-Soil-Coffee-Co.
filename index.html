Our Initiative

Be Their
Village.

Every child in foster care deserves a village of support. We believe that every cup of coffee we pour is a small act of love for the kids and families who need it most.

"It takes a village to raise a child — and we want to be part of that village."
— Good Soil Coffee Co.

Why Foster Care?

There are hundreds of thousands of children in the foster care system waiting for a stable, loving home. Behind every statistic is a real child — a real family navigating an incredibly hard road.

We started Good Soil Coffee Co. not just to pour great coffee, but to pour into a cause we believe in deeply. A portion of every event we serve goes directly to foster care organizations and families in our community.

When you book us for your event, you're not just getting a great coffee experience — you're joining a community of people who believe these kids are worth fighting for.
